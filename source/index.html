ABOUT ME


Cynthia Roberts

Cynthia Roberts is the sole proprietor of Garden Concepts. She is an accomplished gardener with a Certificate in Horticulture, and a life long love for gardening.

Cynthia was awarded the Communities in Bloom Trillium Award for ‘Most Improved’ front yard in 2005 and again in 2014. Cynthia has also hosted three garden tours over the years.

In a professional capacity, Cynthia was responsible for the City of Niagara Falls ‘city wide beautification’ until her retirement in 2013.

As the gateway to Canada, the well maintained streetscapes and beautiful flowerbeds played an integral role in welcoming the world to this first class destination.


MY SERVICES

Garden Concepts provides for all your home and business garden needs! Serving the Niagara Area.

Garden Staging

Selling a home starts at the curb! An appealing, well-maintained garden and landscape, can increase the value of your home by as much as 10%.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Conduct Initial Consultation (Check List and Recommendations)
  • Thoroughly Tidy Existing Landscapes
  • Provide Staging Services and Props (Decorations) Including:
    • Staging Prior to Home Going on Market
    • Staging Prior to an Open House
    • Regular Plant Care Until Sale is Finalized

Landscape Re-Do's

Tired looking and overgrown landscapes can bring down the value of a home. Landscape re-do's are available for front and back yards for any size property.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Conduct Initial Consultation (Evaluation and Recommendations Based on Interests and Lifestyles)
  • Clean Up/Trim Existing Plants (shrubs, perennials, etc)
  • Draw up a Plan Highlighting Recommendations
  • Install Plant and Hardscape Materials
  • Provide Easy Plant Care Instructions

Container Creations

Colourful and attractive flower pots and containers, are a quick and easy way to enhance a front entrance or 'gathering' area at the back yard. Seasonal displays are available.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Utilize Homeowner's Containers or Provide New Containers
  • Plant Containers with Potting Soil and Plant Material
  • Provide Simple Plant Care Instructions Allowing for Optimal Showing
