ABOUT ME


Cynthia Roberts

Cynthia Roberts is the sole proprietor of Garden Concepts. She is an accomplished gardener with a Certificate in Horticulture, and a life long love for gardening.

Cynthia was awarded the Communities in Bloom Trillium Award for ‘Most Improved’ front yard in 2005 and again in 2014. Cynthia has also hosted three garden tours over the years.

In a professional capacity, Cynthia was responsible for the City of Niagara Falls ‘city wide beautification’ until her retirement in 2013.

As the gateway to Canada, the well maintained streetscapes and beautiful flowerbeds played an integral role in welcoming the world to this first class destination.

Landscape re-do's

Tired looking and overgrown landscapes that are 10+ years old are no longer attractive and bring down the value of a home, as well as having an effect on your quality of life and comfortable day-to-day living. Landscape Re-do's are available for front and back yards for any size property.... from condominiums to large estates.

Garden Concepts will:

Conduct an initial consultation, including recommendations, that will take into account the Homeowners interests and lifestyles;

Clean up/trim existing plant material (shrubs, perennials, etc);

Draw up a plan highlighting recommendations;

Install new plant material and hardscape materials (wood chips, rocks, garden art);

Provide easy maintenance instructions for healthy plant life for years to come. (Maintenance provided upon request)

CONTAINER CREATIONS

Colourful and attractive flower pots and containers are a quick and easy way to enhance a front entrance or 'gathering' area at the back yard. Seasonal displays are available.

Garden Concepts will:

Utilize existing containers provided by Homeowner or provide new ones;

Plant containers using special potting soil mix and plant material  determined in consultation with Homeowner;

Add slow release fertilizer;

Provide simple instructions to keep planter looking beautiful and healthy for several weeks.
