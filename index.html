About Us

Garden Concepts sprouted seed in 2015 with a focus on 'garden staging' - providing curb appeal prior to selling a home.

It grew and flourished to include all things gardening - garden makeovers; for residential and businesses, garden maintenance for Spring and Fall clean-up and/or regular weekly/monthly maintenance; as well as uniquely designed porch pots and artificial arrangements for all seasons.

Cynthia and Susan employ their love for gardening and their creative skills towards an exclusive garden experience.

Whether the client wants to sell their home and needs a quick tidy-up with wow-power, is looking for a fresh look to their tired and overgrown flower beds; requires assistance with garden clean-up in Spring and/or Fall, or regular weekly/monthly garden maintenance, Garden Concepts can deliver!

Homeowners with limited gardening experience or busy lifestyles will benefit from all that Garden Concepts has to offer.

Cynthia Roberts

Cynthia is an accomplished gardener with a Certificate in Horticulture, and a life long love for gardening.

Cynthia was awarded the Communities in Bloom Trillium Award for ‘Most Improved’ front yard in 2005 and again in 2014. Cynthia has also hosted three garden tours over the years.

In a professional capacity, Cynthia was responsible for the City of Niagara Falls ‘city wide beautification’ until her retirement in 2013.

As the gateway to Canada, the well maintained streetscapes and beautiful flowerbeds played an integral role in welcoming the world to this first class destination.


Susan Clark

Susan's love affair with "all things gardening" began more than 30 years ago, while accompanying her mother on a garden tour in England and Scotland.

As well as looking after her own gardens with the usual micro-climate challenges, Susan spent four years working in a Niagara area greenhouse … a true labour of love.

The greenhouse specialized in succulents, but offered the full range of gardening products – annuals, perennials, tropicals, vegetables, shrubs, grasses as well as mixed planters and patio containers for all seasons.

Services

Garden Concepts provides for all your home and business garden needs! Serving the Niagara Area.

Garden Staging

Selling a home starts at the curb! An appealing, well-maintained garden and landscape, can increase the value of your home by as much as 10%. Garden Staging for special occasions (wedding, anniversary, etc) is also available.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Conduct initial consultation (check list and recommendations)
  • Thoroughly tidy existing landscapes
  • Provide staging services and props (decorations) including:
    • Staging prior to home going on market
    • Staging prior to an open house
    • Regular plant care until sale is finalized

Garden Makeovers

Tired looking and overgrown landscapes can bring down the value of a home. Landscape re-do's are available for front and back yards for any size property.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Conduct initial consultation (evaluation and recommendations based on interests and lifestyles)
  • Clean up/trim existing plants (shrubs, perennials, etc)
  • Draw up a plan highlighting recommendations
  • Install plant and hardscape materials
  • Provide easy plant care instructions

Garden Maintenance

Regular garden maintenance is available for the busy homeowner, or the family that likes to enjoy their outdoor space but lacks the green thumb to keep it looking great. Garden maintenance also available to those going on vacation and do not want to come back to overgrown flowerbeds or dead plants due to lack of water.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Conduct initial consultation based on interests and lifestyles
  • Clean up/trim existing plants (shrubs, perennials, etc)
  • Weed, cultivate and edge gardens

Container Creations

Colourful and attractive flower pots and containers, are a quick and easy way to enhance a front entrance or 'gathering' area at the back yard. Seasonal displays are available.

Garden Concepts Will:

  • Utilize homeowner's containers or provide new containers
  • Plant containers with potting soil and plant material
  • Provide simple plant care instructions allowing for optimal showing
